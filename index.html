Our Process

We handle the reporting.
You run your business.

Simple, low-friction intake. Rigorous methodology. Reports you can hand to anyone with confidence — reviewed and signed off by our team before they reach you.

01

We Gather Your Data

We walk you through a structured, low-effort intake to collect what we need — utility bills, fuel records, travel data, supply chain information. We do the organizing and cleaning.

02

We Run the Accounting

Using our ISO 14064-aligned carbon accounting tool, we calculate your Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions with verified emission factors and fully documented methodology.

03

Our team reviews every figure, flags anomalies, and ensures the analysis meets the quality standard required for external disclosure, investor reporting, or third-party verification.

04

We Deliver Your Reports

You receive audit-ready reports tailored to your needs — investor disclosures, customer documentation, regulatory filings. Clear, credible, and ready to use immediately.

Our Methodology

Backed by the
standards that matter

We don't use black-box calculations or proprietary methods. Our carbon accounting work is built on internationally recognized standards that your customers, investors, and regulators already accept as credible.

Our primary accounting tool is aligned with ISO 14064 — ensuring your emissions inventory is calculated consistently, comparably, and using peer-reviewed emission factor databases.

Why this matters: When a customer's procurement team, an ESG-focused investor, or a regulator asks how your numbers were calculated, you can point to internationally recognized standards — not an internal spreadsheet. That's the difference between a defensible disclosure and a liability.
🌿
ISO 14064 Aligned
The International Standard for GHG quantification and reporting. ISO 14064 compliance is the benchmark for credible, auditable carbon inventories — accepted by investors, supply chains, and regulators worldwide.
📐
GHG Protocol — Scope 1, 2 & 3
We account for the full picture — direct operations, purchased energy, and supply chain — using the GHG Protocol corporate standard alongside ISO 14064.
✅
Audit-Ready & Third-Party Verifiable
Every engagement produces the documentation trail needed for third-party verification — an increasingly required step from investors, enterprise customers, and regulators.
